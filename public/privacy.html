Down for a Cross terms & privacy

Last updated: 12/26/2017

What information do we collect?

When you sign in with facebook, we store a facebook-generated user id to keep track of your identity. This allows you to enjoy Down for a Cross on different devices and enjoy the same experience.

What do we do with this information?

Down for a Cross never supplies your personal information to any third party for advertising or promotional purposes.

Is my information secure?

At its current state, Down for a Cross cannot offer any guarantees with regards to security. We advise against transmitting any sensitive or personal data through the website.
